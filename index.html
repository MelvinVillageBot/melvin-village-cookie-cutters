Our Story

Melvin Village is the kind of place where neighbors wave from their porches, winters bring real snow (not the slushy city kind), and homemade cookies are serious business.

We started this shop to share that small-town warmth with bakers everywhere. Even though we use modern 3D printing technology, every cutter is designed with the heart of a rustic New England kitchen.

Made with Love (and PLA): Our cutters are printed on-demand using food-safe, plant-based plastic. No mass production, just quality tools for your kitchen.
